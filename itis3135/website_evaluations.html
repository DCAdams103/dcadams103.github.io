The crappy website:

Illuminati Exposers

From the get-go you can tell this is an odd website,
with it's tiny description in the top corner stating:
"Destroying man made religions and traditions and
exposing the churches lies. Exposing Satan and his NWO"

  • C: The contrast for this page is a little off, especially with the title of the page being neon blue.
    Further down the page there is yellow and green text.
  • R: This pages layout is repetitive, with text being above or beside an image or youtube link.
  • A: Alignment is off with the images being different sizes, and varies throughout the page.
  • P: Proximity is good, items are close together, maybe a little too close.


The good website:

Nakato Japanese Restaurant and Sushi

A nice restaurant here in Charlotte.
They're specifically a Japanese Steakhouse and Sushi Bar.

  • C: Contrast throughout the page is good. In the header they gave the
    text a colored background to make it easier to read. All the text
    throughout the page is easy to read.
  • R: Repetition throughout the page is also good, having sections that stretch
    all the way across the page, and some sections that consist of three columns.
  • A: Alignment is also good on this page, everything is centered and easy to see.
  • P: Proximity is good on this page, everything is evenly spaced and nothing feels cluttered.
