Application and it's purpose:

Antonio's 3D Modeling Portfolio: Made by Dylan Adams

October 15th, 2021

The website I will be building is a portfolio for my friend Antonio,
showcasing his 3D models and animations.

The 3D Models will all be interactive by using the Library React-Three-Fiber along side ReactJS and Next.JS

The intended users are anyone interested in seeing his art, such as family, friends, or potential employers.

Who is my client?

His name is Antonio, and he is a friend from Boulder, Colorado.

His email is: doscoolcompany@gmail.com

His phone number is private.
